Particles

The July #CodePenChallenge starts now!


This month, we're going to be playing with particles üéÜ

So, what's a particle?


Often, they look like dots or confetti. Sometimes they're
super-tiny. In front end development, "particles"
encompass everything from floating bokeh dots in the
background of a page to interactive "particle systems" with
realistic physical properties.


This month you'll have a chance to try all kinds of different
particle permutations, and we'll hook you up with lots of
ideas and resources to help you build new front-end skills
or flex your particle panache.
